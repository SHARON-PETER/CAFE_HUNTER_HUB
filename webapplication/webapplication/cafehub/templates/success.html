About Cafe Hunter Hub

We're more than just a cafe directory – we're a community of coffee lovers and cafe owners.

Founded in 2024, Cafe Hunter Hub was born from a simple idea: make it easy for everyone to find the perfect coffee spot, while helping local cafes thrive. We integrate interactive maps, provide exclusive vendor coupons, and offer a seamless e-shop for coffee merchandise.

Our platform connects thousands of coffee enthusiasts with over 850 partner cafes across 200+ Indian cities. Whether you're looking for a quiet corner to work, a place to catch up with friends, or the best espresso in town – we've got you covered.

Partner With Cafe Hunter Hub

Join our growing network of cafe partners across India. Increase visibility, attract more customers, and grow your business.

Benefits for Vendors

  • Increased Visibility: Reach thousands of potential customers
  • Targeted Audience: Connect with coffee lovers in your area
  • Reputation Building: Collect and showcase customer reviews
  • Marketing Support: Promotional features on our platform
  • Digital Presence: Complete online profile with photos & menu
  • Business Insights: Analytics and customer behavior data
  • 30-Day Discount Coupon: Special promotion for new registrations
  • Featured on Map: Blue pin highlighting for partner cafes
  • Revenue Sharing: Earn 90% of coupon discount value

Partnership Requirements

  • Registered cafe/coffee shop business
  • Valid business license and GST number
  • Quality products and service standards
  • Regular operating hours
  • High-quality photos of your cafe
  • Willingness to engage with customers online
